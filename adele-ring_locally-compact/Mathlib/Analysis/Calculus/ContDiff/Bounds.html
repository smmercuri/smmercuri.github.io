    Bounds on higher derivatives #

    norm_iteratedFDeriv_comp_le gives the bound n! * C * D ^ n for the n-th derivative of g âˆ˜ f assuming that the derivatives of g are bounded by C and the i-th derivative of f is bounded by D ^ i.
