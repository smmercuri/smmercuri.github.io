    Convexity of functions and derivatives #

    Here we relate convexity of functions ℝ → ℝ to properties of their derivatives.

    Main results #

    • MonotoneOn.convexOn_of_deriv, convexOn_of_deriv2_nonneg : if the derivative of a function is increasing or its second derivative is nonnegative, then the original function is convex.
