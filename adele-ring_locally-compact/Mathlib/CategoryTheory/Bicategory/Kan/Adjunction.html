    Adjunctions as Kan extensions #

    We show that adjunctions are realized as Kan extensions or Kan lifts. We also show that a left adjoint commutes with a left Kan extension.

    References #

    • [Riehl, Category theory in context, Proposition 6.5.2][riehl2017]
