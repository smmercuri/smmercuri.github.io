    (Co)limits of functors out of SingleObj M #

    We characterise (co)limits of shape SingleObj M. Currently only in the category of types.

    Main results #

    • SingleObj.Types.limitEquivFixedPoints: The limit of J : SingleObj G тед Type u is the fixed points of J.obj (SingleObj.star G) under the induced action.

    • SingleObj.Types.colimitEquivQuotient: The colimit of J : SingleObj G тед Type u is the quotient of J.obj (SingleObj.star G) by the induced action.
