    Infinite sums over ℕ and ℤ #

    This file contains lemmas about HasSum, Summable, and tsum applied to the important special cases where the domain is ℕ or ℤ. For instance, we prove the formula ∑ i in range k, f i + ∑' i, f (i + k) = ∑' i, f i, in sum_add_tsum_nat_add, as well as several results relating sums on ℕ and ℤ.
